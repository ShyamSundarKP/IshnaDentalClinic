Good to know

Frequently asked questions

Do I need to pay anything to book online?

No — booking online only reserves your slot. Payment happens at the clinic after your consultation and treatment plan are finalised.

What should I bring for my first visit?

Any prior dental records or X-rays if you have them, and a list of medications you're currently taking. Everything else, we'll handle in-clinic.

Do you treat dental anxiety or nervous patients?

Yes — this is a big part of how the clinic is designed. Tell us when you book and we'll plan a slower, more explained first visit.

Is parking available near the clinic?

Street parking is available directly outside the clinic on 1st Street, G Block, Ranganathan Garden.
